No. 01 psychart.app

Astrology · Engineering · 3D

Psychart

Your birth chart, computed to the arcminute — and honest about what it cannot know.

A premium astrology experience where every position is real astronomy. Planetary longitudes are cross-checked against an independent NASA-derived engine — 14,770 comparisons, worst disagreement a third of an arcminute — and when something can't be known, like a rising sign without a birth time, it says so instead of guessing.

The sky you scroll through is the actual sky: 83,479 real stars with measured parallax distances, rendered in WebGL with physically correct brightness — so the fly-through has true depth, and the constellations match the ones above your head.

No. 02 agrimakis estate

Craft · Brand · Story

Agrimakis Estate

The Golden Drop — organic extra virgin olive oil from Crete.

A cinematic journey from grove to bottle for a family estate on the southern coast of Crete — terraces of old Koroneiki trees three hundred metres above the Libyan Sea, worked the same way for generations: one variety, an early harvest, a same-day cold press.

The oil earns every word on the label: acidity below 0.2%, certified 100% organic by BioHellas, and named among the top 25 in the world at Anuga 2011. The site's job is simply to carry that story faithfully — nothing exaggerated, nothing rushed.
